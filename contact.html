Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For faster assistance, please include your phone number.

How do I report incorrect school data?

Select "Data Correction Request" from the subject dropdown and include the school name, incorrect data point, and source of correct information.

Can I advertise on your site?

Yes! Please select "Advertising / Sponsorship" from the subject dropdown, and our partnerships team will reach out with media kit and options.

Do you offer API access?

API access is available for premium partners. Please select "Partnership Opportunity" for more information.
