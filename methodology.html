School's reading proficiency percentage compared to state average

Schools performing above state average receive higher academic scores.

Student-Teacher Ratio Scoring

Lower student-teacher ratios generally indicate more individualized attention:

  • < 15:1 - Excellent (5 stars)
  • 15:1 - 18:1 - Good (4 stars)
  • 18:1 - 22:1 - Average (3 stars)
  • 22:1 - 25:1 - Below Average (2 stars)
  • > 25:1 - Poor (1 star)

Graduation Rate Scoring

  • 95-100% - Excellent (5 stars)
  • 90-94% - Good (4 stars)
  • 80-89% - Average (3 stars)
  • 70-79% - Below Average (2 stars)
  • < 70% - Poor (1 star)

Parent Review Integration

Parent reviews are validated through our verification system:

  1. Email verification required
  2. Duplicate detection prevents multiple reviews

  4. Reviews contribute 20% to final rating

Frequently Asked Questions

How often is data updated?

We update our database quarterly as new state data becomes available. Parent reviews are updated in real-time.

Can schools request changes?

Yes, school administrators can contact us to report data discrepancies. We verify all correction requests with official sources.

How are charter schools rated?

Charter schools are rated using the same methodology as traditional public schools, with additional weight on their charter-specific performance metrics.

What about private schools?

Private schools are included when data is publicly available. However, due to varying disclosure requirements, private school data may be less comprehensive.

Transparency Note

We are committed to transparency in our ratings. If you have questions about our methodology or would like to suggest improvements, please contact us. We regularly review and refine our algorithm based on user feedback and emerging research in educational assessment.
